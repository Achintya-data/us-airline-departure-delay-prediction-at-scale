Pipeline Scope

Data Engineering

  • Raw to silver to gold storage layout on Google Cloud Storage.
  • Feature-ready train, validation, and test splits persisted for reproducibility.
  • Leakage-safe aggregates and reusable ML-ready feature vectors.

Modeling and Analysis

  • Weighted Logistic Regression, Random Forest, and Gradient Boosted Trees.
  • Ablation study for base, congestion, weather, and full feature sets.
  • Slicing analysis by airport, month, carrier, and time block.

Unsupervised Learning

  • K-Means clustering on standardized operational and weather variables.
  • PCA visualization and cluster profiling.
  • Cluster regimes used as operational interpretation and meta-feature support.

Operational Monitoring

  • Robust z-score anomaly detection across months, airports, and time blocks.
  • Scale-in learning-curve analysis for data ROI.
  • Scale-out Dataproc experiments for cluster efficiency and runtime tradeoffs.

What the Results Say

  • Weather produced the largest performance lift in the ablation study.
  • The model plateaued around 25% of the training data for this setup.
  • Adding workers beyond three did not improve runtime for the scale-out benchmark.
  • The project emphasizes cost-effective experimentation, not just bigger clusters.
